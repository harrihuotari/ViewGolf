Golf results of Harri and his friends

Enter results and see your earlier golf scores

First make sure you are listed as a player of the system. If the course is missing in the list, please add it before the golf round. You can use also Android mobile application for storing the results.

Golf results in data base

The results are stored to database. There might be several players taking part in the event, having different tee times (starting time of the round). The actual handicap is used for counting the slope for the player. The database includes also tables for the courses, holes of the courses, players and scores.

Available selections

The available selections to access the data through this page are listed on top of this page.
Events will give you a list of events where basic information, like the course and the date are shown.
Players will show you a list of players and some basic information of them.
Rounds will select a page where you can choose an event and a player, and then you will get the result (score card).
New score is used for storing the results.
From 'Courses' current list of courses can be seen and new courses added.
